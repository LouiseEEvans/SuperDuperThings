Welcome to Super Duper. Make yourself at home!
Here you can find original graphic artwork, prints, notebooks and general paper goods. All these Super Duper things are designed and made by me, Louise Evans, a graphic designer living and working in London, UK.

I’m most interested in playful, colourful things that will make you and your walls feel happy. Shapes, colours texture - often inspired by nature, or stories, my prints are at home in any room of your house and span all ages.

I am also completely in love with books and paper, so you’ll find handcrafted notebooks and even the odd special zine project here too. And lots of lovely shiny copper!
